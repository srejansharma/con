The Famous Paya

Paya is a thick, creamy, meaty soup prepared from legs of lamb. The best place to have Paya in the country is in Bhopal, from where it originated, but it certainly is available across the country.

Pomfret Fish Recipe

Chingri Macher Malaikari

An extremely popular Bengali curry, Prawn Malai Curry is a celebratory recipe and is usually referred to as ‘Chingri Macher Malaikari’ by most of the Bengalis.

Dum Chicken
